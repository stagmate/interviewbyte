# Product Directory Submission Guide

This guide provides all the information needed to submit InterviewMate to major product directories for backlinks and discoverability.

## Core Product Information

### Basic Info
- **Product Name**: InterviewMate
- **Website**: https://interviewmate.tech
- **Tagline**: Real-Time AI Interview Coach for Live Video Calls
- **Category**: Productivity, AI Tools, Career Tools, Interview Preparation
- **Pricing**: Freemium ($10 for 10 credits, no subscription)

### Description (Short - 160 chars)
Real-time AI assistant for live video interviews. Get instant answer suggestions in 2 seconds while interviewing on Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet.

### Description (Long - 500 chars)
InterviewMate is a real-time AI interview assistant that helps you DURING actual live video interviews with recruiters. Unlike practice platforms, it works while you're on Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet calls with real interviewers. Upload your resume and experience, and get personalized STAR-format answer suggestions in under 2 seconds using Deepgram Flux and Claude 3.5 Sonnet. Designed specifically for behavioral interviews at big tech companies like Google, Amazon, and Microsoft.

### Key Features
- Real-time speech-to-text with sub-second latency (Deepgram Flux)
- AI-powered answer generation in 2 seconds (Claude 3.5 Sonnet)
- Works during live Zoom, Teams, Google Meet interviews
- Personalized answers based on your resume and experience
- STAR method optimization for behavioral interviews
- No practice mode - designed for actual interviews only
- Privacy-first: No recordings stored

### Tech Stack
- Frontend: Next.js 14, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
- Backend: FastAPI, Python
- AI: Claude 3.5 Sonnet (Anthropic)
- Speech-to-Text: Deepgram Flux
- Vector DB: Qdrant
- Deployment: Vercel (frontend), Railway (backend)

### Target Audience
- Job seekers interviewing at tech companies
- Non-native English speakers
- Professionals preparing for behavioral interviews
- Candidates interviewing with Google, Amazon, Microsoft, Meta

### Competitors & Differentiation
- **vs Practice Platforms**: Works during REAL interviews, not mock interviews
- **vs ChatGPT**: 2-second response time, personalized to your experience
- **vs Generic AI Tools**: Optimized specifically for STAR behavioral interviews
